Our mission is to provide students the opportunity to experience and learn more about computer technology within a motivated community.

SST Computer Club encourages students to learn more about computer technology and engineering through the development of technical skills and collaboration with other like-minded students. Through SST Computer Club, students can:

  • Learn new programming languages/tools
  • Build their own projects (for their portfolios/resumes)
  • Participate in tech events/challenges/competitions
  • Collaborate with teams
  • Have fun in a comfortable environment (LAN parties)

We tend to meet after-school, at 2:30-3:30, in the computer lab.

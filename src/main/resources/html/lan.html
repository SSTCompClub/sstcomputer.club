  2. LAN Parties

Each year SST Computer Club hosts a total of 5 LAN parties. We charge attendees $5 to get in and have some fun, but we've had it fluctuate up to $10. All profit that's made is mainly put into getting food and drinks for the LAN, with the remaining proceeds going to Computer Club itself.

The food that we serve for this event is generally pizza, ranging from vegetarian, to pepperoni, cheese, and meat & veggie pizzas. For drinks we serve Mountain Dew, but there is generally water available as well.

Our LAN Parties start at 2:30 p.m. after school, to as late as 7:30 p.m.. The main games that we play at our LAN parties are Team Fortress 2, Dota 2, and Leauge of Legends, but some play what ever game they please to.

The proceeds that we get to keep is planned to be used for competitions and events, or else to improve our LAN parties.

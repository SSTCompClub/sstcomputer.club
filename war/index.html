The start of our own website

by Jasper C.

Greetings everyone.

For everything we do, we have a purpose behind it for why we do it. Today our purpose is to create a central point for our club, where we all can see what's going on and has been planned, as well to store files for projects and competitions we may be doing, then we have who we are and our history.

It has been a great pleasure working with all of you. Pass on the knowledge, and may our future generations master technology even better than us.

We have also uploaded the code of website on GitHub. What we have right now is what we'll ue as our starting point, and from there on we shall continue to complete this website.
